Rules

At the moment there are no regulations on my website concerning killing times. All previously uploaded combat logs since the Rift 4.5 update are counted and are shown in the fastest kill time list.
Sometimes I find it necessary to filter out DPS values in the top 15 list. In this case, the DPS values are not deleted but only no longer displayed in the top lists. There are many ways to unnaturally increase the DPS of individual players. To prevent abuse, there are a few rules that must be followed to be displayed in the Top 15 list.

1. no second lava field buff or flaring power buff inerhaled for 5 minutes
2. no stacking of several Primal savagery buffs from the mystic.
3. no skipping of defensive spin (Azranel)
