About this project.

The site was created to get a quick overview of the current meta and to collect ideas for new raidsetups. Also because stats are fun and we like turtles. The algorithm calculates the time and the single target damage from the first hit on a NPC until he is dead or doesn't take any more damage, so you get more realistic st dps values and more precise kill times. If you have suggestions for improvements you can post them in the Forum. Keep feeding the turtle!
